## 1.0.0 — 2026-07-01
Rebranded from marketing-psychology (marketingskills, MIT).

### Improve pass (/refract)
- Added **selection discipline**: diagnose the behavioral obstacle first and recommend the
  2-3 highest-leverage models rather than dumping the ~70-model catalog (the original invited
  enumeration; this makes the skill act on its own Paradox-of-Choice warning).
- Added an **ethics gate** before any persuasion tactic (genuine scarcity only, easy opt-out,
  no exploiting vulnerable states, truthful framing) — a bias catalog is dual-use.
- Added a **"When these backfire"** section documenting where each family of tactics misfires
  (fake scarcity, nudge fatigue, discount-trained customers, hollow social proof) — omitted
  by the source.

### Network hook
- share-with-tribe: after a psychology recommendation, offers (never auto-posts) to surface it
  to the user's Luckiest tribe, with a private-by-nature exclusion.

### Trend pass (/newsjack + /luckiest-trends)
- no actionable signal (foundational mental models are Lindy-stable; the live-social trends
  engine requires interactive first-run setup unavailable in this pass, and no dated,
  attributable source was obtained — nothing fabricated to fill the pass).
